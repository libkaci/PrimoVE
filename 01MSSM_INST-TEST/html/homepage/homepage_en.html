Research Discovery provides simple, one-stop searching for journals, articles, books, e-books, digital media, and more.

Our new platform also helps you customize and manage your research experience.

When you sign up for a Research Discovery Account you will have access to the following features:

  • Renew books and materials
  • Create and save your favorites lists
  • Export citations to your Reference Management System
  • View full search results (Some databases, only show results when you're signed in.)

Mount Sinai students, faculty and staff do not need a Research Discovery Account to access and view full-text resources, but you can sign up for an account to gain access to personalized features.
